Profile

Robotics engineer (M.Sc. Automation & Control, RPTU Kaiserslautern; B.Sc. Mechatronics, UET Lahore). Currently a Wissenschaftlicher Mitarbeiter at RPTU deploying an autonomous John Deere tractor for precision weed cutting, and commissioning a Unitree G1 humanoid in Brussels.

Background spans humanoid locomotion and sim-to-real, manipulation on UR5e / Tiago platforms (MoveIt, Robotiq, 6D pose estimation), agricultural field robotics with multi-sensor fusion under degraded conditions, and LLM / VLM-grounded robot behaviour (GPT-3.5 Turbo, LLaMA, Gemini Flash). Looking to extend hands-on work into formal research on mobile manipulation for humanoids — integrating whole-body control, perception, and foundation-model-driven planning on real hardware. Designed and fabricated collaborative robots from scratch.
