John Deere — Automated Kreisel

Commissioning a fully automated kreisel system on a John Deere 5105-ML tractor — under real outdoor field conditions, with a focus on precision weed cutting.

Field robotics Autonomy Sensor fusion ROS 2 Docker

Highlights

  • Fully automated Kreisel system on a John Deere 5105-ML tractor.
  • GPS / IMU sensor-fusion pipeline reaching < 4 cm position error under fixed RTK and ~12 cm under degraded RTK — characterized failure modes across the signal-quality envelope.
  • ROS 2 Docker stack with continuous telemetry and off-site diagnostics for resilient headless operation.
